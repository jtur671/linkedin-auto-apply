how it works

Setup (one time, takes ~2 min)

1

Click the Download button above

2

Open the downloaded file from your Downloads folder

3

Windows will warn you — click "More info" then "Run anyway"

4

A black window opens and installs everything. Don't close it.

5

When it's done, your browser opens the dashboard automatically

Keep the black window open while using the app. If you close it, the app stops. To restart, just double-click the file again.

Once running, your dashboard is at:

localhost:3000
